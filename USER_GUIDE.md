## User Role Guide

As a standard user, your primary goal is to manage your business's finances, including clients, invoices, and expenses.

### Dashboard
The **Dashboard** gives you a quick overview of your financial health.
- **Key Metrics:** View total revenue, outstanding invoices, and expenses at a glance.
- **Recent Activity:** See the latest invoices and payments.
- **Quick Actions:** Buttons to quickly create a new invoice or add a client.

### Client Management
Navigate to the **Clients** section to manage your customer base.
- **Add Client:** Click "Add New Client" to store details like name, email, phone, and address.
- **View Client:** Click on a client to see their profile and transaction history.
- **Edit/Delete:** Update client information or remove them from your records.
- **Search:** Use the search bar to find clients by name or email.

### Invoice Management
The **Invoices** section is the core of the application.
- **Create Invoice:**
    1. Select a client.
    2. Set issue and due dates.
    3. Add line items (description, quantity, price).
    4. Save as Draft or Send immediately.
- **Invoice Statuses:**
    - **Draft:** Saved but not finalized.
    - **Sent:** Emailed to the client.
    - **Paid:** Payment has been recorded.
    - **Overdue:** Past the due date without payment.
- **Actions:**
    - **Send Email:** Email the invoice PDF directly to the client.
    - **Mark as Paid:** Record a payment manually.
    - **Download PDF:** Generate a professional PDF version of the invoice.

### Expense Tracking
Keep track of your business spending in the **Expenses** section.
- **Add Expense:** Record amounts, dates, and categories (e.g., Office Supplies, Travel).
- **Categories:** Organize expenses for better reporting.
- **Receipts:** Attach notes or references to expenses.

### Reports
Gain insights into your business performance.
- **Revenue Report:** Track income over time.
- **Expense Report:** Analyze spending patterns.
- **Profit & Loss:** See your net income (Revenue - Expenses).
- **Export:** Download reports as PDF for accounting purposes.

## Admin Role Guide

Administrators have full access to the system and are responsible for managing users and system-wide settings.

### Admin Dashboard
The **Admin Dashboard** provides a high-level view of the entire platform's usage.
- **Total Users:** Monitor user growth.
- **System Health:** Check for any system-wide alerts.

### User Management
Navigate to **Users** to manage accounts.
- **View Users:** See a list of all registered users.
- **Edit Roles:** Promote users to Admin or demote them to standard users.
- **Delete Users:** Remove accounts if necessary.

### System Settings
Configure global application settings.
- **General Settings:** Application name, logo, and default currency.
- **Email Settings:** Configure SMTP settings for sending emails.
- **Tax Settings:** Set default tax rates.
